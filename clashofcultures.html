Fictions - Translating and Ideas

Sketching

During the entire project I drew a lot to come up with further ideas. My idea for this concept: four clans compete against each other per season, one vs one per event night. The intention is that everyone has been against each other, with this you can vote which two subgenres fit the best. In addition, there is also one special event where two famous artists compete against each other, this will take place in the season finale. A winner is selected by draw and can access all events for free next season.

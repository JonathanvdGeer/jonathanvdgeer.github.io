Prototype - InDesign

VoorKeur

Our concept is about a planning tool for doctors where elderly people get medical examinations for their driving licenses. Older people are less able to drive at a later age, which means that they must pass an inspection to show that they can still drive. The problem is not with the elderly, but with the doctors themselves. They are getting a new planning tool app, but a few functions are still missing. Together with the client, we looked at and investigated the missing functions. Based on all kinds of research, we have designed an extension of the application, where doctors can read their newsletters and ask or answer their questions (forum).

Product Bio - Notion

Leading up to the problem

Streaming services already offer various albums or playlists of old music, but you lack the visual feeling to give your collection its own place. In addition, I experience with almost all music streaming services that little is done with old music carriers. To make a link between music streaming services and old music carriers, you can make a re-experience in today's digital world, where music from different music carriers can be easily shared and you can create playlists in a digital environment.

That is why I want to develop a central place to collect music and to create a digital re-experience of old music carriers, where you can easily collect and share music with friends. All elements of such a physical music carrier, such as the experience of sound and design, must be reflected in the app in order to experience the nostalgic feeling.

The challenge is to develop an app that is distinctive from the existing music streaming services, where physical core brands are digitally implemented. The app is for people who are familiar with different music carriers and want to collect all their music in one digital environment. This makes it easier to collect and share both old and new music in one environment.

About

Myself

Hi, my name is Jonathan van de Geer and I am 25 years old. I recently completed in 2022 my Bachelor of Science "Communication & Multimedia Design" education at the University of Applied Sciences Amsterdam.

It’s a nice challenge for me to be creative as a designer. I see myself as a User Experience and Interface Designer. As a User Interface designer I design the interface of websites, apps or other digital platforms. I like to make designs for different clients and like to improve the user experience. In addition, I want to apply and improve my skills from my current and previous education as well, so that I can combine my learning skills with internships and work.

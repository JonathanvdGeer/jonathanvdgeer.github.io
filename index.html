About

Myself

Hi, my name is Jonathan van de Geer and I am 23 years old. Currently I am in my last phase of my 3rd year of Bachelor's Degree "Communication & Multimedia Design" at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam.

It’s a nice challenge for me to be creative as a designer. I like to make designs for different clients and I like to improve the user experience. In addition, I also want to apply and improve my skills from my current and previous education, so that I can combine my learning skills with internships and work.

Past and current courses

  • Communication and Multimedia Design [HBO]

    In the CMD course I am focussing on the entire design process, from understanding a design issue to realizing the end product. For this I make use of the knowledge I gain in technology, design and interaction design subjects. I am learning how to make the best choices for my design.

  • Designing User Research Minor [HBO]

    Challenges me to investigate different research methods. In addition to theoretical research, the emphasis will be on field-, laboratory- and workshop research and generative research methods such as probes, co-creation and diaries, searched for meaningful data (qualitative and quantitative).

  • The School of House - Dance, Music and Events Course

    The School of House provides a unique combination of theory and practice, where prominent guest speakers from the Dance industry play a key role. DJs, producers and organisers – from pioneers to young guns, and from underground to mainstream – talk passionately about their world.

  • Events, Marketing and Communication [MBO4]

    Event marketing focuses on developing, deepening and expanding a relationship with one or more (invited) target groups, whereby the event or series of events are used as a marketing and communication tool and where emotion and experience come together.
